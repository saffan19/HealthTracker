How it works
Track daily diet & exercise for Afrah and Safwan — Jul 6 to Oct 10.
1
Log your day
Use the big Today buttons, or tap any cell in the grid. Each tap flips between 1 and 0. 1 = diet was good / you exercised, 0 = not. Blank (–) means nothing logged yet.
2
Read your stats
Healthy % is the share of your logged days where diet = 1. Active % is the same for exercise. Streaks count consecutive days (up to today) at 1.
3
Share between both of you
Click Connect GitHub once and paste a repo + access token. Your data saves to a data.json file you both share. Press Sync to pull the other person's latest changes.
4
Works offline too
Without connecting, everything still saves in this browser. Use Export anytime to download the raw JSON.
Connect GitHub
Point at a repo with a data.json file. Create a fine-grained token with Contents: Read & write on that repo. Stored only in this browser.
