How our tracker works
Little healthy habits, all the way to October 22 — together.
Check it off
In Today’s check-in, tap the circle under Diet if you ate well and Move if you exercised — it fills with a checkmark. Tap again to undo. Empty just means not yet.
Get cheered on
Every check brings a little heart celebration; undoing one gets a gentle, kind pep-talk — no guilt here. Your cheer squad rotates their notes every time.
Missed a day? All good
Scroll to any date in Our daily log and tap it in later. Watch your Healthy, Active and streaks climb toward the big day.
Shared just for you two
You’re both on the same page automatically — whatever one logs, the other sees. It saves as you tap and refreshes on its own; hit Sync for the latest now.
